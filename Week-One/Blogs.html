Cats, scientifically known as Felis catus, are one of the most popular and beloved pets worldwide. Renowned for their grace, independence, and mysterious charm, cats have captivated humans for centuries. With their sleek bodies, sharp claws, and keen senses, cats are natural-born hunters. Even domesticated cats exhibit their hunting instincts through playful pouncing, chasing, and stalking behaviors. However, behind their predatory nature lies a softer side. Cats are known for their affectionate behavior towards their human companions. They often form strong bonds with their owners, providing comfort and companionship.
